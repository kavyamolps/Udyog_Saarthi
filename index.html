    About Us

    The Udyog Saarthi is a great idea to help Persons with Disabilities(PwD's) to find employement opportunities. It would be a valuable resource for job seekers,job coaches,and employers.

    Objectives:-
    • It is a dedicated job portal for Persons with Disabilities(PwD's).
    • It provide information about Government ,Public and Private sector real-time job openings across India.
    • The platform also provides online courses to those who have intrest and for those who have lack of skill.
    • The persons who are using this platform can use this platform as a job portal and at the same time as an education platform.
    Benefits:-

    Will ensure maximum employability of PwD's as per current market requirements.
